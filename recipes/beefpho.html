Beef Pho

View on allrecipes.com

Description

Pho is a Vietnamese noodle soup that features rice noodles, herbs, and a healthy serving of protein. By adding hot sauce, bean sprouts, mints, and green onions, you can make beef pho taste amazing.

Ingredients

  • 5 pounds beef soup bones
  • 1 tablespoon salt, divided
  • 2 gallons water
  • 2 medium onions, quartered
  • 2 medium onions, quartered
  • 1 (4 inch) piece fresh ginger root
  • 2 pounds beef oxtail
  • 1 white (daikon) radish, sliced
  • 2 ounces whole star anise pods
  • 2 whole cloves
  • 1 tablespoon white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon fish sauce
  • 1 ½ pounds dried flat rice noodles
  • ½ pound frozen beef sirloin
  • Salt to taste

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Make broth, using beef bones, salt, and water. Let it simmer for about 2 hours.
  3. Preheat the broiler and broil the onions and ginger for 10-15 minutes.
  4. Chop the onions and peel the ginger.
  5. Add aromatics and spices to the broth and simmer for at least 4 hours.
  6. Drain the broth and discard the bones used. Chill the broth for at least 8 hours.
  7. Skim and discard fat. Prepare the noodles and meat.
  8. Add the broth to a small bowl of the noodles and meat. Enjoy!

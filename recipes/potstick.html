Pot Stickers

Description

Potstickers are dumplings believed to be originated from China. They are typically filled with pork, cabbage, and other ingredients. They're usually pan-fried and have a soft, tender filling with a crunchy dumpling outside.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground pork
  • 4 cloves minced garlic
  • ½ cup finely chopped green onions
  • 3 tablespoons very finely minced fresh ginger
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 teaspon sesame oil
  • 1 pinch cayenne pepper
  • 1 ½ cups finely chopped green cabbage
  • 2 ½ cups all-purpose flour, or more as needed

Steps

  1. Mix ground pork, green onions, garlic, ginger, soysauce, sesame oil, and cayenne in a bowl and top with green cabbage. Chill for an hour.
  2. Mix flour and salt with water to form a dough and let it rest for 30 minutes.
  3. Roll dough into thin circles to form the pot sticker wrappers.
  4. Add filling into the circles. Moisten the edge of the circles with water and pinch the dumpling closed.
  5. Add dumplings to a pan and cook until golden brown. Serve with a rice vinegar and soy sauce dipping sauce. Enjoy!
